# EcoVolt-AI-Smart-Energy-Optimization

EcoVolt AI is a smart energy optimization platform designed for educational institutions. The system monitors energy consumption, analyzes occupancy patterns, predicts energy wastage using AI concepts, and provides intelligent recommendations to improve energy efficiency and sustainability.

---

## 🌍 Problem Statement

Educational institutions consume large amounts of electricity every day. However, a significant portion of this energy is wasted because:

- Lights remain ON in empty classrooms.
- Air conditioners operate without occupancy awareness.
- Computer labs consume energy during idle periods.
- Institutions lack intelligent systems to predict and prevent energy waste.

This results in:

- Increased electricity bills
- Higher carbon emissions
- Inefficient resource utilization
- Reduced sustainability performance

---

## 💡 Solution

EcoVolt AI provides an intelligent energy management platform that:

- Monitors occupancy levels in real time
- Calculates energy consumption dynamically
- Predicts energy wastage probability
- Generates AI-driven recommendations
- Tracks efficiency scores
- Visualizes analytics through interactive dashboards
- Supports sustainability initiatives and SDG goals
