THE CHALLENGE

Energy Wastage in Education

Educational institutions consume enormous amounts of electricity daily, yet a significant portion is wasted due to poor occupancy management and lack of intelligent monitoring systems. Lights, fans, air conditioners, and computers remain active in empty classrooms, driving up costs and carbon emissions — undermining sustainability goals worldwide.

💡

Unused Lighting Systems

Lights remain on in unoccupied classrooms and corridors for hours, wasting energy with no benefit to anyone.

~35% of lighting energy wasted
❄️

AC Overconsumption

Air conditioning systems run at full capacity regardless of room occupancy or outdoor temperature conditions.

~42% of HVAC energy wasted
💻

Device Energy Waste

Computer labs and smart boards remain powered on overnight and during holidays without any productive use.

~28% of device energy wasted
📈

Rising Electricity Bills

Unchecked consumption leads to skyrocketing utility costs, diverting institutional funds away from education.

₹12 Lakh+ annual loss
